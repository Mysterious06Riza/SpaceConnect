Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about SpaceConnect.

What is SpaceConnect?

+

SpaceConnect is a platform that connects ideas, portfolio, and hobbies into one space. It serves as a creative hub for sharing projects, articles, and various interests.

Is SpaceConnect free to use?

+

Yes, SpaceConnect is completely free to use. Some premium features may require a subscription or one-time payment.

Who is behind SpaceConnect?

+

SpaceConnect was created by Ali Almansoor, a data scientist and AI engineer who is also passionate about astronomy and science.

What is Project AXIOM?

+

Project AXIOM is a scientific initiative founded by Ali Almansoor. More details about the project can be found in the Articles section.

Do I need an account to use SpaceConnect?

+

While most content is freely accessible without an account, creating an account gives you access to personalized features, commenting, and the ability to save favorite content.

How do I create an account?

+

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page, and follow the prompts to create your account using your email or social media profiles.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

+

On the login page, click on "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How often is new content added?

+

New content is added on a regular basis. Major updates typically happen weekly, while smaller updates may occur more frequently.

Can I contribute content to SpaceConnect?

+

Yes, we welcome contributions! Please visit the "Support" page to learn how you can submit your articles, projects, or other content for consideration.

Which browsers are supported?

+

SpaceConnect works best with modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

+

Yes, SpaceConnect is designed to be responsive and works well on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers.

How can I get help with an issue?

+

Visit our "Support" page to find contact information, submit a support ticket, or browse common solutions to known issues.

What is the e-magazine mentioned in the footer?

+

The free yearly e-magazine is a digital publication containing highlights, articles, and special content. You can subscribe to it through the QR code in the footer.
